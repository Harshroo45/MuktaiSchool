Residential Facility

Safe and nurturing boarding facilities with trained caregivers, providing a home-like environment.

Education

Customized learning programs focused on basic life skills, literacy, and numeracy adapted to individual abilities.

Health & Nutrition

Regular health checkups, nutritious meals, and wellness programs ensuring overall physical health.

नियमित स्वास्थ्य तपासणी, पौष्टिक अन्न आणि कल्याण कार्यक्रम।
